Dart 1.0 Dart Flight School

Are you ready to fly with Dart?

We’ll provide the materials you need to get your community onboarded with Dart. Here are some of the formats we will support for your Dart Flight School event:

  • Tech Talk: Host a talk to learn more about what makes Dart a First-Class language.
  • Code Lab: Go from runway to take-off through a guided hands-on Code Lab.
  • Hackathon: Create your own flight plan and use Dart to bring your ideas to the web.

Be part of a global event

  • Get Tier-One Dart support during the event.

  • Get your event spotlighted by the Google Developers G+ page & Dart's G+ page.
